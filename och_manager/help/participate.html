Who can participate?

Any a cappella congregation in the Church of Christ tradition is welcome to participate.

We ask that instrumental congregations not participate. This is for purely practical reasons: instrumental services often add quite different sets of songs, and it's simply not feasible for us to keep up with the additional repertoire.

If your church has multiple services, some instrumental and some a cappella, you can enter just the a cappella services.
