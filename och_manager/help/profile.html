Sharing worship history

If you uncheck the "Share worship history data with other congregations" box, your congregation will not appear in any summaries or datasets that are visible to other congregations. (You also won't be able to see other congregations' data.)

Basic congregation info

Please fill out general information about your congregation: name, website (if you have one), address.

An email address is required, but it doesn't have to be the official email of your congregation. This is the email that will receive a code if you need to reset your password; choose an email that's accessible to whoever will be entering data.

Other interesting data

We're collecting information about a few other characteristics of congregations that might end up being related to the types of songs they sing.

  • Congregation size: Approximate Sunday morning attendance.
  • Praise team since: When, if ever, did your congregation start using a praise team on Sunday mornings? (We assume that congregations rarely stop using a praise team once they've started. If your congregation has in fact done this, please let us know and we'll modify how this data is collected.)
  • Women leading worship since: When, if ever, did women start participating in public roles in Sunday morning worship at your congregation? (We think this is less likely to be directly related to singing practices than the previous two items. But it's interesting, and you never know!)
