Welcome to Observing Congregational Hymnody!

What is this?

This site records songs sung in worship among the Churches of Christ. Its goal is to help congregations learn more about their own singing practices and about singing in their sister congregations.

How does it work?

Each participating congregation gets a login. Enter your worship history—that is, a record of which songs you have sung on which Sundays. The site will show you a summary of the types of songs your congregation sings, as well as a comparison with other congregations.

The songs entered here link to a comprehensive database of C of C hymnody (see here for more information and here for a searchable interface). This site shows basic summaries by topic, year the song was written, and more; you can also download the data and join to that database for whatever further analysis you're interested in.
